Creamy Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 3 Tbs Butter
  • 2 tbs Milk
  • Johnnys Seasoning Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat pan on medium low heat
  2. Crack eggs into a small bowl and add milk
  3. Place butter into heated pan. Pan will be ready once the butter is melting but not popping
  4. Place egg/milk mixture into pan
  5. Stir constantly using a rubber spatula
  6. after 45 seconds sprinkle your seasoning salt
  7. Continue stirring until eggs are fluffy and golden
  8. Enjoy!
