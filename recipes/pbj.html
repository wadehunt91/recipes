How to make a PB&J Sandwich

Your guide on how to make the perfect peanutbutter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Sourdough Bread
  • Jiffy Creamy Peanutbutter
  • Smuckers Red Rasperry Jam

Steps

  1. Toast the two slices of bread in the toaster until medium brown
  2. Let the toast cool unil slightly warm to the touch
  3. Apply Peanutbutter to bread slice #1
  4. Apply Jam to bread Slice #1
  5. Put the two peiced of bread together with the ingrediants facing each other
  6. Take a Butcher knife and cut off the crusts aroung all four edges
  7. Cut the remaining bread diagonally starting at the top right of the sandwich and moving toward the lowere left hand corner of the sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
